Corrugated PP vs Solid PP Bins

Corrugated PP bins offer better performance per pound than solid-wall alternatives:

  • 30–50% lighter than equivalent solid PP bins
  • Same load capacity due to flute geometry
  • Lower material cost — less PP required per unit
  • Easier to die-cut and fold for custom shapes
  • FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 food-safe
